Cristiano Ronaldo

'The best the world has ever seen'

A brief rundown of his ongoing legacy...

  • 1985 - He was born on February 5th on the island of Madeira, Portugal
  • 1997 - At age 12, he was signed onto Sporting Lisbon for a fee of £1500
  • 2001 - At 16, he was promoted to Sporting's youth team, being the first player to play for the U16, 17, 18, B, & A team all within a single season
  • 2002 - He made his debut in the Portugese Premier League against Braga
  • 2003 - Signed to Manchester United by Sir Alex Ferguson for £12.24 million after impressing with a win over United
  • 2007 - Won his first Premier League title, earning the "Premier League Player of the Month" twice in a row
  • 2008 - Carried United to win the FIFA Club World Cup, earning himself the Ballon d'Or and the "FIFA World Player of the Year" award
  • 2009 - Inaugurated for the FIFA Puskas Award and signed for Real Madrid for £80 million, a world record at the time
  • 2010 - Given his iconic number 7 jersey following Raul's departure
  • 2013 - His contract to Real Madrid was renewed for £17 million, making him the highest-paid football player as well as receiving the FIFA Ballon d'Or
  • 2014 - During the World Cup qualifiers, he became the all-time top goalscorer for the Portugal national team & the UEFA European Championship
  • 2015 - Became the all-time top scorer for Real Madrid as well in the Champions League
  • 2016 - Despite injury, led his team to winning the UEFA Euros, achieving the "Silver Boot" and having Madeira airport renamed to "Cristiano Ronaldo International"
  • 2018 - Signed with Italian club Juventus for a transfer fee of £100 million, the highest ever for a player over 30
  • 2021 - Re-signed with Manchester United for £12.85 million causing record breaking t-shirt sales during the first 24 hours after the transfer
